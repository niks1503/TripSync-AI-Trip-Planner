Safety & Cultural Tips for Maharashtra:
1. Taxis/Autos: Always insist on the meter in Mumbai/Pune. Use Uber/Ola for safety.
2. Street Food: Delicious but ensure it's freshly cooked. Avoid cut fruits on the roadside.
3. Night Safety: Mumbai is generally safe at night, but exercise caution in isolated areas.
4. Language: Marathi is the local language. Hindi and English are widely understood.
5. Festivals: Crowds during Ganesh Chaturthi and Govinda can be overwhelming. Keep valuables safe.
6. Scams: Be wary of unauthorized tour guides at monuments. Use official guides.
