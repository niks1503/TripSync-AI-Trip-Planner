Temple Rules & Etiquette in Maharashtra:
1. Dress Code: Shoulders and knees must be covered. Avoid sleeveless tops and shorts.
2. Footwear: Remove shoes before entering the inner sanctum or temple premises.
3. Photography: Often prohibited inside the main shrine. Check signs before clicking.
4. Offerings: Flowers and coconuts are common. Do not offer non-vegetarian items.
5. Silence: Maintain silence and respect the devotees praying.
6. Menstruation Rule: Traditionally, women are advised not to enter during menstruation in some temples (e.g., Shani Shingnapur), though rules are modernizing.
